About Priority Donor Club

We're a life-saving platform that connects blood donors with recipients in need across communities.

Our mission is to make blood donation as accessible as possible, ensuring no one suffers due to lack of donors.

Founded in 2023, we're committed to creating connections that save lives through voluntary blood donation.

Our Impact

  • Connected over 10,000 donors with recipients
  • Reduced blood shortage wait times by 60%
  • Built a community of 5,000+ regular donors
